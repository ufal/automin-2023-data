11 6.11. Toys Directive ***I (vote)

Report: on the proposal for a directive of the European Parliament and of
the Council on the safety of toys [COM(2008)0009 - C6-0039/2008 -
2008/0018(COD)] - Committee on the Internal Market and Consumer Protection.
Rapporteur: Marianne Thyssen (A6-0441/2008)

(Simple majority)

(Voting record: 'Results of votes', Item 11)

The following spoke: Herbert Reul and Hannes Swoboda, on the voting
procedure (the President replied that their comments would be passed on to
the Conference of Presidents), Evelyne Gebhardt, who requested that the
Commission make a statement before the final vote, Jacques Barrot (Vice-
President of the Commission), Hannes Swoboda, who requested that the House
proceed to the vote but that the final vote be held over until a later
date, and Jacques Toubon, who requested that the final vote be held that
day as planned.

By electronic vote (354 in favour, 187 against, 12 abstentions) Parliament
decided to proceed to the vote, including the final vote.

The following spoke: Bernd Posselt.
