- The meeting discusses the emission performance standards for new passenger cars.
- Members agree that first-reading agreements should be rare and only used in exceptional circumstances.
- There is no legal provision for the involvement of Heads of State in the codecision process.
- The unique and transformative nature of EU ETS legislation is emphasized, but consultation with Heads of State and Government should not be taken as a precedent for other legislation.
- The European Parliament has been cooperative with the Council, but the decision-making process should be an exception rather than the rule.
