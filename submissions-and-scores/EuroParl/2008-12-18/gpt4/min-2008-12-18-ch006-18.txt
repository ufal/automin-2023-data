- The draft general budget for 2009 was discussed and modified by the Council.
- The rapporteur, Jutta Haug, expressed the Parliament's willingness to enter into negotiations with the Council on the EUR 5 billion proposed by the Commission for the revision of the MFF 2007-2013.
- The European Economic Recovery Plan was mentioned in the context of the negotiations.
- The situation of the European schools in Brussels was discussed, with concerns raised about the delayed opening of the fourth school in Laeken and the current enrolment procedure.
- The Commission was expected to present a revised enrolment procedure by the end of March 2009, with objective and comprehensible criteria.
