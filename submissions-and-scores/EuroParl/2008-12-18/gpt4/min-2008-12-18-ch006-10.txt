- A delegation from the Syrian Parliament attended the 10th EP/Syria Interparliamentary Meeting
- The delegation was led by Mr. Suleiman Haddad, Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Syrian People's Assembly
- The Syria-EU Association Agreement was recently initialled and will be submitted to the European Parliament for approval
- The European Parliament believes Syria can play a positive role in the Middle East and the Mediterranean Union
- The meeting aimed for a fruitful exchange of views to improve relations between the parties
