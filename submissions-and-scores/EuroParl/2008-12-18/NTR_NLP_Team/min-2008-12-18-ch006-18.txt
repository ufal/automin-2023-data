Meeting KeyPoints:

- The EU has a humanitarian obligation to offer protection to people fleeing war, persecution and other violent conditions.
- The EU must develop an efficient system of external migration management, including without distinction regarding grounds, safe and legal channels for movement.
- Border management should be a joint exercise of the Member States under the responsibility of the EU.
- A common approach to return and readmission agreements should be agreed at EU level.
- EU cooperation with third countries should be based on international obligations.
