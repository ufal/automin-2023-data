- The Council discussed the Draft General Budget 2009 and modified it
- Amendment 1 was proposed by the rapporteur, Jutta Haug, expressing Parliament's willingness to enter into negotiations with the Council on the basis of the Commission's proposal for a revision of the MFF 2007-2013 for EUR 5 billion in the framework of the proposed European Economic Recovery Plan
- Amendment 2 was proposed by the rapporteur, Jutta Haug, concerning the situation of present and future young pupils in the European Schools in Brussels
- The Commission was expected to present a revised enrolment procedure by the end of March 2009 with objective and comprehensible criteria
- Both amendments were accepted
- Robert Goebbels expressed his approval of the outcome
