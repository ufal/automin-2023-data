- The final version of the draft agenda was distributed at the Conference of Presidents meeting on Thursday, 15 October 2009.
- Monday's agenda includes a vote without debate on a report by Mrs Wallis concerning the immunity of Mr Siwiec.
- Tuesday's agenda has no amendments proposed.
- Wednesday's agenda includes a request from the Group of the European People's Party (Christian Democrats) for an amendment to the title of the draft resolutions on freedom of information in Italy and other Member States of the European Union.
- Thursday's agenda includes a discussion on the terrible death penalties in Iran.
