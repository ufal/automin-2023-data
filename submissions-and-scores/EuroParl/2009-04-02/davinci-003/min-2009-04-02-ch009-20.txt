- Meeting was held to discuss the application of Directive 2004/38/EC on the right of EU citizens and their family members to move and reside freely within the territory of the Member States
- Adina-Ioana Vălean proposed an oral amendment to footnote 1 to recital S, first indent, to delete the last two sentences and insert 'IT' (Italy) in the sequence at the beginning of the paragraph
- Stefano Zappalà proposed that the resolution be further amended and then referred back to committee for re-examination
- Roberta Angelilli clarified that the questionnaire sent to the Member States contained incorrect terminology
- 40 Members had to rise to oppose the motion, and no explanation would be given
- Parliament agreed to accept the oral amendment
