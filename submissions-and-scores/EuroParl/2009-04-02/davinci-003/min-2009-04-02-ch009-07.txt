- A request was made to postpone the final vote on the resolution and report on the Interim Trade Agreement with Turkmenistan until the next plenary sitting
- The request was made due to the lack of guarantees from the Commission and the Council that, if the human rights situation continues to worsen, serious consideration will be given to the suspension of the agreement
- The request was made by the Group of the European People's Party (Christian Democrats) and European Democrats
- Parliament decided to postpone the final vote
- The vote on the amendments to the report on Turkmenistan was accepted
- An oral amendment was accepted to replace 'the prospect of signing the Agreement' with 'the prospect of finalising the process of ratifying the Agreement'
