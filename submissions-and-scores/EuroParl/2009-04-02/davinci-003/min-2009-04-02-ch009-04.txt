- Extensive urbanisation in Spain has had a huge effect on the lives of tens of thousands of EU citizens
- The European Parliament has committed itself to respecting the fundamental rights of European citizens
- The report has been approved by the Committee on Petitions by a two-thirds majority
- The report addresses the impact of urbanisation on the environment, individual rights, and the application of EU law
- The report calls for a proper debate on the issue and for changes to rules that prevent this
- The report calls for respect for the environment and cultural heritage, and for appropriate compensation for victims of property scandals
