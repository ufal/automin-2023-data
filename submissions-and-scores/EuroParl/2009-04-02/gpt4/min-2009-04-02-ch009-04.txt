- The Auken report discusses the impact of extensive urbanisation in Spain on individual rights of European citizens, the environment, and the application of EU law.
- The report is the result of extensive work in the Committee on Petitions, involving Members from all political groups.
- The report addresses the problem of fundamental rights of European citizens, including the right to lawfully acquired property.
- It also discusses the disastrous impact of extensive urbanisation on the environment, particularly in coastal areas and on the Spanish islands.
- The report calls for legal certainty and appropriate compensation for the victims of property scandals in Spain.
