- Meeting discusses the application of Directive 2004/38/EC on the right of EU citizens and their family members to move and reside freely within the territory of the Member States.
- Rapporteur Adina-Ioana Vălean proposes an oral amendment to footnote 1 to recital S, first indent, to delete the last two sentences and insert 'IT' (Italy) in the sequence at the beginning of the paragraph.
- Stefano Zappalà appreciates the suggestion but believes the resolution contains too many references and should be further amended and referred back to the committee for re-examination.
- Roberta Angelilli agrees with Vălean's amendment but points out that the questionnaire sent to Member States contained incorrect terminology regarding the right to free movement of second, third, and fourth wives.
- Parliament agrees to accept the oral amendment.
