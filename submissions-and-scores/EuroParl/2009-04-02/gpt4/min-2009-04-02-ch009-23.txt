- Meeting discussing the adoption of the Ecolabel Regulation
- Commission confirms intention to propose a regulation on ecolabelling of fishery products before the end of the year
- Regulation to be mainly based on criteria for sustainable fishing
- Study in Article 6(5)(a) of the Ecolabel Regulation will examine additional aspects such as processing, pre-packaging, packaging, and transport
- The study will not influence or prejudge the adoption of the regulation
