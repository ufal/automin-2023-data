- Christa Prets proposes an oral amendment to delete the word 'legal' in points 5, 8, and 16.
- Prets argues that the term 'legal' could exclude unregistered children or those involved in asylum procedures from education programs.
- Prets emphasizes that every child has the right to education if registered in a country.
- Stavros Lambrinidis supports the deletion of the word 'legal' in paragraph 8 as well.
- Parliament does not agree to accept the oral amendments.
