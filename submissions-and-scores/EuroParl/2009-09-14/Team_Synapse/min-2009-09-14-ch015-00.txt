SUMMARY
- The Conference of Presidents has voted to place the election of the President of the Commission on Wednesday's agenda.
- On Tuesday afternoon they will hold a debate on the President's proposals.
- The representative of the non-attached Members has still not been appointed.
- The referendum in Ireland is due to be held in 22 days' time.
- This referendum is crucial to the acceptance or rejection of the Treaty of Lisbon.
- Cohn-Bendit discussed a motion put forward by his group in the Conference of Presidents last week.
- The Council wants to elect the President on the basis of the Treaty of Nice, whereas the European Union claims to be based on applicable law.
- The European Parliament wants the Treaty to be applied.
- The President of the Commission should be elected on a different legal basis from the Commissioners.
- The Parliament should be ready to function under Treaty of Lisbon and then the European Parliament will be able to vote on Mr Barroso.
