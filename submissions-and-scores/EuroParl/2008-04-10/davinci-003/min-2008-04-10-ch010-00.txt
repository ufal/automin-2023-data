- A delegation from Australia's House of Representatives and Senate is taking part in the 31st EP-Australia interparliamentary meeting
- The delegation is led by Senator Alan Ferguson, President of the Australian Senate
- The European Parliament and the Australian Parliament began their direct and wide-ranging political dialogue in 1981
- The meeting is being held in the European Parliament on 9 and 10 April
- The President of the European Parliament welcomed the delegation and wished them success for the remainder of their stay
- The President also wished them a safe journey home when the time comes
