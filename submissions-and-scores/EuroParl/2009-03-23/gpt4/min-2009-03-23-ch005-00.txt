- The meeting discussed the progress report for the Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia's accession process.
- Erik Meijer, the author of the resolution, made three technical remarks before the vote.
- First, he clarified the correct title for the Macedonian negotiator in paragraph 12.
- Second, he proposed surrounding the name "Macedonian Orthodox Church" with inverted commas in paragraph 18.
- Third, he suggested using the original English version for translations of the word "pending" in paragraph 10.
- Giorgos Dimitrakopoulos requested a roll-call vote on the second part of paragraph 12.
- Anna Ibrisagic proposed a deletion of one part of the text in paragraph 13.
- Hannes Swoboda accepted Ibrisagic's proposed wording and withdrew his Amendment 3.
