DATE : 2023-05-02
ATTENDEES : PERSON006


SUMMARY
- There was a partial collapse of the false ceiling in the plenary chamber in Strasbourg on 7 August.
- The first part-session of the European Parliament had to be held in Brussels.
- The investigations are ongoing and are being carried out by several independent international structural engineering firms commissioned by Parliament.
- The apportionment of responsibility and liability between the contractors involved in the construction of the original ceiling will be established on the basis of the final investigation reports.
