3. Tribute

The President paid tribute on Parliament's behalf to the memory of the
Members Bronis?aw Geremek, who had died on 13.07.2008, and Willi Piecyk,
who had died on 01.08.2008, as well as to the memory of former Member Maria
Luisa Cassanmagnago, who had died on 04.08.2008.

He expressed his sadness at the tragic aeroplane accidents that had
occurred at Madrid-Barajas airport on 20.08.2008 and at the airport of the
Kyrgyz capital Bishkek on 24.08.2008, in which many people died.

Parliament observed a minute's silence in memory of all those who had died.
