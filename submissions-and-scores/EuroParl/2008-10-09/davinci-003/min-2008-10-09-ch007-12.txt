- PPE-DE Group requested the postponement of the vote on the resolution regarding the governance of the International Accounting Standards Board
- Reason for the postponement is to allow for new proposals from the European Commission to be considered
- Financial crisis has highlighted the importance of accounting standards and their governance
- European Commission has drafted a proposal without consulting the Council or the European Parliament
- European Parliament has proposed to reopen the discussion if the Commission agrees to reconsider its proposal
- Commission has not expressed an opinion
- Parliament adopted the motion for postponement of the vote
