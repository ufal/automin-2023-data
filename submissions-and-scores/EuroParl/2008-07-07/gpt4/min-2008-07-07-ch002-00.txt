- Godfrey Bloom raises concerns about rapporteur Caroline Jackson receiving undeclared money from a recycling company while working on the committee.
- President acknowledges the seriousness of the claim but cannot comment on the circumstances.
- Caroline Jackson defends herself, stating she made a full declaration and has always been open about her involvement with the company.
- President vouches for Jackson's personal integrity and confirms her declaration is properly noted in the Register of Members' Financial Interests.
- Monica Frassoni calls for better regulation on conflicts of interest to ensure transparency and correctness in the Parliament.
