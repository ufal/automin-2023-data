- Erasmus Mundus Programme (2009-2013) was signed by the Council, President, and Commissioner Figel'
- Programme is an extension of the original Erasmus Programme established 21 years ago
- Programme gives students around the world the chance to study in the European Union
- Nearly EUR 950 million in funding for five years
- Programme includes grants for students and professors from third countries, doctoral programmes, and increased financial support for European students
- Programme promotes openness and intercultural dialogue
- Thanks to the French Presidency, Commission, and Committee on Culture and Education for their work on the legislative measure
