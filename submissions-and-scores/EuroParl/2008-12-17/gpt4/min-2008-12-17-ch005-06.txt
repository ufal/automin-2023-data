- Erasmus Mundus action programme signed for enhancement of quality in higher education and promotion of intercultural understanding through cooperation with third countries.
- Extends the original Erasmus programme established 21 years ago.
- Offers students worldwide the opportunity to study in the European Union.
- Increased funding of nearly EUR 950 million for five years to meet growing demand.
- Scope of the programme extended to include doctoral programmes and more financial support for European students.
