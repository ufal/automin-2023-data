- Christopher Beazley addressed the Vice-President of the Commission regarding a resolution on the EU-Russia Summit.
- The resolution was passed by Parliament's Baltic Europe Intergroup a month ago.
- Beazley expressed concern over not receiving an acknowledgement or reply.
- Margot Wallström, Vice-President of the Commission, promised to follow up on the issue.
- Wallström assured Beazley that a reply would be provided as soon as possible.
