- A delegation of MPs and other visitors from the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia and the Mission of Mongolia to the European Union have taken their seats in the distinguished visitors' gallery. 
- The members of the delegation are in Brussels for the 7th European Union-Mongolia Interparliamentary meeting. 
- The meeting has strengthened the important and growing relationship between the European Parliament and the Mongolian Parliament. 
- The visitors have had an opportunity to experience the workings of the European Parliament. 
- The President wished the delegation well for the remainder of their stay in the European Union.
