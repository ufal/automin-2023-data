• Equal treatment and access for men and women in the performing arts is still an issue.
• There is a lack of female directors in theatre, dance, and opera.
• Auditions behind a screen should be widened to recruit orchestral musicians to combat discrimination.
• Balance between professional and family commitments is difficult for women in this sector due to unusual working hours.
• Crèches should have specific opening hours to meet the needs of artists.
• Europe cannot continue to do nothing in the face of these problems.
