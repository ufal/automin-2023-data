- Directive 2006/43/EC on statutory audits of annual accounts and consolidated accounts was discussed
- The report by Mr Doorn on implementation of the directive was presented
- The report calls on the Commission to take steps quickly to correct deficiencies in transposal of the directive
- The report questions whether the method of harmonisation employed is the right way to attain uniform application of key concepts
- The report discusses the Commission's recommendation on the limitation of the civil liability of accountants
- The Commission continuously monitors the transposition status of the directive in the Member States
- The Commission has adopted the recommendation on quality assurance and the recommendation on the limitation on the civil liability of statutory auditors
- The Commission will assess the national laws transposing the directive and inform Parliament of the effects of the measures taken by the Member States
- The vote will take place the following day
