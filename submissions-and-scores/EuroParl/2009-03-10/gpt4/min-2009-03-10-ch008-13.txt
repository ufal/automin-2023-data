- Meeting discussed the implementation of Directive 2006/43/EC on statutory audits of annual accounts and consolidated accounts
- Many issues were found in the transposal of the eighth directive, including key definitions being framed differently in different countries
- Report calls on the Commission to quickly correct these deficiencies
- Questions were raised about whether the method of harmonisation employed (minimum harmonisation) is the right way to attain uniform application of key concepts
- The economic crisis highlighted the importance of strict auditing and oversight
