- The meeting discussed the urgent procedure for a proposal for a Council regulation establishing a multi-annual recovery plan for bluefin tuna in the Eastern Atlantic and Mediterranean.
- The proposal aims to implement a new recovery plan recommended by the International Commission for the Conservation of Atlantic Tunas.
- The regulation needs to come into force before the main fishing season starts on 15 April.
- The European Parliament's Committee on Fisheries unanimously approved the request for the application of the urgent procedure.
