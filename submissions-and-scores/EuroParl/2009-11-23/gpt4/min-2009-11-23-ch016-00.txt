- Isabelle Durant reminds attendees about the Lux Prize, a film prize to be awarded.
- Attendees have the opportunity and obligation to vote for one of the three films in the competition.
- A program on the films being shown and a DVD of the 2007 winning film are provided in a file.
- Attendees are asked to watch the films and vote, either on the website or on channel 77 on their television.
- The Lux Prize is a citizens' initiative promoting European values and European filmmakers, and attendees have one week left to vote.
