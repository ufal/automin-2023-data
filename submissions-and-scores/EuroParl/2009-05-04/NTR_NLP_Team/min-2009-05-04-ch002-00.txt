Meeting KeyPoints:

In five years since the enlargement, we in the European Parliament, and the other institutions of the European Union, have integrated 12 new members. We have learnt to meet each other half way and work together more closely. However, a larger and more diverse EU requires closer cooperation and the a greater capacity to take action.
