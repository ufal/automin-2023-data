- Meeting discusses the vote on a report regarding tax evasion linked to import and cross-border transactions.
- Political agreement with the Council was in place to adopt a number of amendments for the regulation.
- Due to the absence of over 400 MEPs, the required qualified majority of 393 votes was not reached.
- The situation has led to a broken agreement with the Council.
- The Bureau and Parliament's administration will look into how to salvage the situation before the recess after 7 May.
