2. Statements by the President

The President made a statement on the occasion of the fifth anniversary of
the EU's enlargement to include ten new countries in 2004.

                                      
                                      

The President made a statement expressing his anxiety at the fate of the
American-Iranian journalist Roxana Saberi, currently imprisoned in Iran. On
behalf of Parliament, he added his voice to those of the President-in-
Office of the Council and of the President of the USA in calling on the
Iranian authorities for the immediate and unconditional release of Roxana
Saberi.

He also protested against the execution in Iran of a woman for a crime
committed when she was still a minor.



                                      
                                      

The President expressed Parliament's solidarity with the Queen of the
Netherlands and with the Dutch people following the incident in Apeldoorn
on 30 April 2009 in which several people died..
