- European Parliament endorses establishment of a contact-point network against corruption
- Network aims to foster cooperation among authorities and agencies in Member States and encourage exchange of data on effective strategies
- Parliament proposes significant amendments, including mandatory participation of OLAF, Europol, and Eurojust in the network
- Network should submit an annual report to the European Parliament on its activities and proposals to improve prevention and combating of corruption
- Costs of network members should be borne from the Community budget to ensure independence
