- The meeting discussed the urgent procedure request for a Council regulation on the conclusion of the Protocol for fishing opportunities and financial contribution in the Fisheries Partnership Agreement between the European Community and the Islamic Republic of Mauritania.
- Philippe Morillon explained the renegotiation of the practical arrangements for the implementation of the partnership agreement with the Republic of Mauritania.
- The renegotiation resulted in the setting up of a Joint Committee, which reached an agreement that satisfies both the European Union and the Republic of Mauritania.
- The goal is to pay the emoluments due to the Republic of Mauritania from 1 August.
- The request for urgent procedure was rejected by Parliament.
