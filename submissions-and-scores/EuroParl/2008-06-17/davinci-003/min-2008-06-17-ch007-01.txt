- Summarize the meeting:
- The fisheries sector is facing a crisis due to increasing diesel prices and poor management of fisheries resources.
- Illegal, unreported and unregulated fishing is a lucrative activity that is damaging fish resources and marine ecosystems.
- The European Commission and the Committee on Fisheries have proposed a system to combat this activity.
- The report was adopted unanimously in the Committee on Fisheries.
- The Council and Member States are urged to take responsibility and implement necessary measures.
