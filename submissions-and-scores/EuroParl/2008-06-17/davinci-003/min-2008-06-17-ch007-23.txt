• At the beginning of voting, fellow members attempted to use the unplanned event at the Krško nuclear power station as an illustration of the unsafe performance of nuclear power plants. 
• Romana Jordan Cizelj pointed out that the event was indeed unplanned, that it involved a component failure, that the plant was not shut down but rather had its operations safely suspended, and that repairs on it are under way. 
• The operation of the power station is transparent and fellow members can find information on it on the website of the country's nuclear safety administration. 
• Romana Jordan Cizelj also added that by all performance indicators the Krško nuclear power station is one of the safest nuclear power plants in the world.
