ATTENDEES : PERSON147


SUMMARY
- The current legislation on residue control has led to a serious decrease in the availability of authorised veterinary medicines.
- The incoming French President should make this dossier a priority for the incoming French government.
- There is an urgent need to review the Veterinary Medicines Directive according to the outcome of the task force on availability of medicines.
