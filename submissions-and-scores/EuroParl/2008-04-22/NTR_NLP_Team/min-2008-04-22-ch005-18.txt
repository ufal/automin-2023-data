Meeting KeyPoints:

Herbert Bösch, chairman of the Committee on Budgetary Control, speaks during a meeting of the European Parliament in Brussels, Belgium. The meeting is to discuss draft discharge procedures for the European Commission, the European Court of Auditors and the European Ombudsman.
