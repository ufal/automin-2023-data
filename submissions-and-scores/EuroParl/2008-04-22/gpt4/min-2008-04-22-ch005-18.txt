- Meeting discussing the 2006 discharge of the European Foundation for the Improvement of Living and Working Conditions.
- Chairman of the Committee on Budgetary Control, Herbert Bösch, informs the chamber that resolutions on discharge of the agencies will be adopted without explanatory statements.
- Bösch deleted the explanatory statement in accordance with Rule 48 of the Rules of Procedure, as it bore no relation to the text passed by the committee.
- Rapporteur Hans-Peter Martin disagrees with Bösch's decision, arguing that the explanatory statement did relate to points of criticism.
- Martin accuses Bösch of applying a double standard in the handling of the explanatory statement.
