- European Parliament meeting discussing the Statute of the European Ombudsman
- Anneli Jäätteenmäki, rapporteur, proposes a procedure agreed upon by the Committee on Constitutional Affairs
- Vote to only relate to a draft decision, with a motion for a resolution postponed until the Commission delivers its opinion and Council approval
- President of Parliament to submit the text of the draft decision agreed by Parliament to the Commission and Council for examination
- Request the Committee on Constitutional Affairs to make necessary contact with the Council and Commission and propose how to proceed in the matter
- Parliament approves the proposal on the voting procedure
