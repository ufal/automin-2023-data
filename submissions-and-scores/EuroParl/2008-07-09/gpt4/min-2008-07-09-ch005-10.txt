- The Commission thanks Mr. Duff for his report on reforming the hearing procedure.
- The Commission supports Parliament's decision to increase transparency and democratic legitimacy in the appointment procedure.
- The Commission emphasizes the need to respect the relevant provisions of the Treaties and the Framework Agreement.
- The procedures for portfolio reshuffles should be lighter than those for approving the whole Commission and should respect the prerogatives of the President of the European Commission.
- The Commission reserves its right to agree on the relevant procedures for different scenarios on a case-by-case basis, in a constructive spirit of cooperation.
