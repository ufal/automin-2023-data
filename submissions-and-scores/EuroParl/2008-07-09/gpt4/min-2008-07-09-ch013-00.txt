- The MEETS program aims to modernize European enterprise and trade statistics.
- It contributes to the process of deregulation and reduction of red tape.
- The report requires the Commission to report annually to Parliament on the progress of deregulation.
- Parliament can play a more prominent role in the process, particularly through committee work.
- The Committee on Economic and Monetary Affairs can take the lead in these matters, engaging in dialogue with Commissioner Verheugen and the Stoiber group.
