DATE : 2023-05-02
ATTENDEES : PERSON099


SUMMARY
- PERSON099 proposes a minor alteration to Article 5 of the EU's constitution.
- The current text reads: "Calls on the EU to encourage the creation of an African Partnership Forum engaging all major donors and investors, namely China".
- Following the amendment, it will read: "The EU should ensure that the African Partnership forum engages all major Donors and Investors, including China".
- The amendment has been communicated to all colleagues and shadow rapporteurs.
