SUMMARY
- The ALDE Group wants to refer the Kirkhope report on the definition of a parent carrier back to the Committee on Transport.
- The Commission is working on a formal notice, but that does not give 100% clarity at this moment.
- There is no rush to put the matter to the vote at this very moment.
- The first-reading agreement was struck in June with Council and Commission, after comprehensive debate and support in the Transport Committee.
