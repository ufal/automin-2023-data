- Marco Cappato proposed two amendments to the resolution on hangings in Iran, related to the UN moratorium on the death penalty.
- The first amendment calls for a resolution at the next UN General Assembly, requesting countries with the death penalty to make information on capital punishment and executions available to the UN Secretary-General and public opinion.
- The second amendment calls for a new resolution to establish a Special Envoy of the Secretary-General to monitor the situation, ensure transparency in the capital punishment system, and promote an internal process for implementing the UN resolution on the moratorium on executions.
- Parliament agreed to accept the oral amendment.
- Other oral amendments were discussed, with some being accepted and others rejected.
