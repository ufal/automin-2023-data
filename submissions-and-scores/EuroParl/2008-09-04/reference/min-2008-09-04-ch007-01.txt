1 7.1. Code of Conduct for computerised reservation systems ***I (vote)

Report: on the proposal for a regulation of the European Parliament and of
the Council on a Code of Conduct for computerised reservation systems
[COM(2007)0709 - C6-0418/2007 - 2007/0243(COD)] - Committee on Transport
and Tourism. Rapporteur: Timothy Kirkhope (A6-0248/2008)

(Simple majority)

(Voting record: 'Results of votes', Item 1)

The following spoke: Jeanine Hennis-Plasschaert, on behalf of the ALDE
Group, who requested that the text be referred back to the committee
pursuant to Rule 168(2), and, on that request, Brian Simpson, on behalf of
the PSE Group, Georg Jarzembowski, on behalf of the PPE-DE Group, and
Timothy Kirkhope (rapporteur).

Parliament rejected the request by electronic vote (265 in favour, 300
against, 37 abstentions).

The text was therefore put to the vote.
