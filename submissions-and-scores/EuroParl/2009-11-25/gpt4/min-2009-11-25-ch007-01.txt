- European Parliament members discussed the importance of implementing European values and guidelines in negotiations with the United States to ensure the security of European citizens.
- The European Crime Prevention Network was discussed, with emphasis on its further development and reform.
- The issue of people-trafficking was raised, with a call for open discussion and strategies to eradicate it.
- The Treaty of Lisbon was mentioned as a means to deepen cooperation and ensure the security of citizens throughout the Member States.
- The importance of a common legal framework and network of highly-qualified forensic laboratories across the European Union was discussed.
