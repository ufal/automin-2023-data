- Meeting discusses the adaptation of the European Parliament's Rules of Procedure to the Lisbon Treaty.
- Bruno Gollnisch raises a point of order based on Rule 24(2) of the Rules of Procedure.
- Gollnisch explains that non-attached Members should appoint one of their own as a delegate to the Conference of Presidents.
- Amendment 86, which has been withdrawn, would have allowed the President to decide which representative of the non-attached Members should sit in the Conference of Presidents.
- Rapporteur David Martin states that the issue will be voted on in January and recommends that the President invite a Non-Inscrit to attend the Conference of Presidents.
