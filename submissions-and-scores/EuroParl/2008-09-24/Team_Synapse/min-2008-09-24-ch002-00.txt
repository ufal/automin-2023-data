SUMMARY
- Eleven people were killed and a number injured in a school shooting in Kauhajoki in the west of Finland.
- The perpetrator then turned the gun on himself.
- The victims were innocent young students at a vocational training school studying for careers in the service sector.
- This happened less than one year after a similar killing spree at Jokela High School.
