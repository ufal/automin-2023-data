- Tragic incident in Kauhajoki, Finland, resulted in 11 deaths and several serious injuries at a vocational training school.
- The perpetrator turned the gun on himself after the attack.
- European Parliament expresses deep sympathy and solidarity with the families and relatives of the victims.
- The tragedy occurred less than a year after a similar incident at Jokela High School.
- Politicians must work to detect and prevent such acts of violence in the future.
