- On behalf of the European Parliament, expressed deepest sympathy and solidarity with the families and all the relatives of the victims of the tragic incident in Kauhajoki, Finland
- 11 people lost their lives and a number of people were seriously injured by the perpetrator
- Victims were innocent young students at a vocational training school
- Finland is regarded as one of the most peaceful and safest countries in Europe
- Similar patterns are often repeated in these acts of bloodshed
- Responsible politicians must do their utmost to ensure that such acts of violence are detected and prevented in time
