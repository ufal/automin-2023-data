- The Committee on Civil Liberties visited 26 detention centers in 10 EU Member States
- Observed state of decay and lack of hygiene in certain centers
- Denounced failure to respect human dignity
- Access to healthcare and children's right to education not always respected
- Called for respect for clear, fair, effective and proportionate asylum procedures
- Called for implementation of permanent system of visits and inspections of detention centers
- Collective responsibility for what goes on in detention centers in Europe
- Called for European solidarity in the area of asylum
