Meeting KeyPoints:

a) Supports Taiwan's participation as an observer in relevant international organisations where this does not require statehood, for instance, in the ILO

b) Also supports Taiwan's application to join the WTO, where it does not require statehood, subject to the WTO's usual demands for consultation and commitment to a rigorous, rules-based trading system

c) Respects the one-China policy and urges all parties to the negotiation of a two-China settlement to follow
