- European SMEs represent 99% of all European companies and 75 million jobs.
- Only 3% of these SMEs access the foreign, external market and only 8% access the internal market.
- The European Parliament is asking the Council and the Commission to consider these facts in negotiations and market access.
- Trade is considered the key to greater prosperity.
- The issue must be tackled without delay to help resolve the economic and financial crisis affecting workers and citizens.
