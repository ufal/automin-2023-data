DATE : 2023-05-02
ATTENDEES : PERSON068, PERSON069


SUMMARY
- PERSON068 and the shadow rapporteurs visited no fewer than 26 detention centres in 10 EU Member States.
- They found an obvious state of decay and an obvious lack of hygiene in some of the centres.
- They denounced the failure to respect human dignity and access to healthcare.
- They also found that children's right to education was not always respected.
