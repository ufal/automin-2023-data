- Summarize the meeting

- Meeting was about the structure and rates of excise duty applied on manufactured tobacco
- The rapporteur asked fellow Members to vote responsibly and to consider a moderate, but definite price increase above the minimum level
- The Committee on Economic and Monetary Affairs adopted the voting suggestion made by the rapporteur
- The rapporteur asked all fellow Members to act responsibly and in such a way that a large majority opinion can be given to the Council
- The goal is to avoid the same thing happening as with the subject of alcohol, where Parliament did not give an opinion
