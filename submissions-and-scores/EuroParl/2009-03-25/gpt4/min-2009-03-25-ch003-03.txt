- Gyula Hegyi, rapporteur, advised Socialist members of the Committee on Culture to abstain from the final vote on the report.
- Hegyi was unsatisfied with the outcome of the votes on the amendments.
- Many fresh and pioneering ideas were voted out by other political groups.
- Hegyi initially planned to abstain from the plenary vote but was convinced by colleagues to support the report.
- Hegyi hopes there will be a chance to improve the report in the future.
