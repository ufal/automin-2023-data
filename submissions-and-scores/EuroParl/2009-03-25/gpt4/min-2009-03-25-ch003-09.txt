- The meeting discussed the structure and rates of excise duty applied on manufactured tobacco.
- The rapporteur, Zsolt László Becsey, emphasized the importance of a responsible vote on the matter.
- The discussion considered a price increase from a health perspective, among other factors.
- The rapporteur warned against a price increase that could lead to increased smuggling or be unsustainable for certain Member States.
- Becsey asked members to consider a moderate price increase above the minimum level and to follow the voting suggestion adopted by the Committee on Economic and Monetary Affairs.
