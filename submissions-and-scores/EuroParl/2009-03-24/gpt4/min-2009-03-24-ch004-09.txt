- 5th World Water Forum took place in Istanbul from 16-22 March 2009
- The Zaragoza 2008 International Expo closed on 14 September 2008
- The expo was the first exhibition devoted exclusively to water and sustainable development
- The European Parliament participated in the expo on an equal footing with the Commission
- The Zaragoza Charter, which reflects the state of the debate between Mexico 2006 and Istanbul, was created after three months of debates with over 2,000 experts and NGOs
