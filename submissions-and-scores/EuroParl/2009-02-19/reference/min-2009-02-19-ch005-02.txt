2 5.2. The petitions process (Amendment of Title VIII of the Rules of
Procedure) (Rule 131) (vote)

Report: on revision of the Rules of Procedure with regard to the petitions
process [2006/2209(REG)] - Committee on Constitutional Affairs. Rapporteur:
Grard Onesta (A6-0027/2009)

The following spoke: Brian Crowley, on behalf of the UEN Group, who
requested, under Rule 170, that the vote be held over until the March II
part-session, and Grard Onesta (rapporteur).

Parliament agreed to the request.
