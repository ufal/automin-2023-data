5 9.5. Financing of actions other than Official Development Assistance in
countries falling under Regulation (EC) 1905/2006 (vote)

Report: with recommendations to the Commission on financing of actions
other than Official Development Assistance in countries falling under
Regulation (EC) No 1905/2006 [2008/2117(INI)] - Committee on Development.
Rapporteur: Thijs Berman (A6-0036/2009)

The following spoke: Thijs Berman (rapporteur), who, on behalf of the PSE
Group, requested that the report be referred back to the committee under
Rule 168(2), and, on that request, Michael Gahler and Ana Maria Gomes.

Parliament agreed to the request.
