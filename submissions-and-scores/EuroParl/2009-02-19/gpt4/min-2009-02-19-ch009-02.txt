- Polish engineer Piotr Stańczak was murdered by terrorists in Pakistan; the European Parliament expressed outrage and condolences to his family.
- Terrorism is a direct attack on freedom, human rights, and democracy, and poses a great danger to the security and stability of the international community.
- The European Parliament expressed solidarity with Australia after the tragic loss of life due to forest fires and sent a delegation to convey condolences personally.
- The European Parliament called on Iranian authorities to respect human rights and the rights of religious minorities, and to reconsider the indictment against seven leaders of the Bahá'í religious community.
- The European Parliament condemned the arrest and expulsion of Spanish Member Mr. Herrero by the Venezuelan Government, calling it an infringement of human rights and a denigration of the democratic institution.
