DATE : 2023-05-02
ATTENDEES : PERSON143


SUMMARY
- The Commission thanks Mr Duff for his report on the reform of the appointment procedure.
- The Commission considers that the procedures to be applied in the different cases covered by the report have to fully respect the relevant provisions of the Treaties and the Framework Agreement.
- The procedures applied for portfolio reshuffles should be lighter than those applied for approving the whole Commission.
