1. Opening of the sitting

The sitting opened at 9.00.

                                      
                                      

The following spoke: Daniel Cohn-Bendit, on the provisions governing the
taking of photographs in the Chamber on the occasion of the visit of
Nicolas Sarkozy, President-in-Office of the Council, and Astrid Lulling
(Quaestor) on the difficulties experienced by certain Members in accessing
Parliament's premises arising from the increased security measures in place
for that visit.
