SUMMARY
- Zaragoza 2008 International Expo closed on 14 September 2008.
- It was the first exhibition devoted exclusively to water and sustainable development.
- The European Parliament took part on an equal footing with the Commission.
- The three months of constant debates, with more than 2 000 experts and NGOs, resulted in the Zaragoza Charter.
