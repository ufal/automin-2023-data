- A vote was held on paragraph 4 of the progress report on Turkey for 2008
- The ALDE Group proposed inserting the adjective 'secular' into the phrase 'stable, democratic, pluralist and prosperous society'
- The Verts/ALE Group proposed adding the word 'transitional' to the amendment, so that it would read 'except for temporary, transitional derogations'
- The Verts/ALE Group also proposed replacing the word 'involve' with 'consult'
- The vote was successful and the amendments were accepted
