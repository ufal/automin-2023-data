- Guidelines for the 2010 budget procedure - Section III, Commission was discussed
- An oral amendment was proposed to paragraph 13, which was distributed and read by László Surján
- The amendment changed the text to read '[...] as well as internal security, particularly the fight against terrorism; immigration, demographic challenges [...]' instead of the old version '[...] as well as internal security, meaning particularly immigration, the fight against terrorism, demographic challenges [...]'
- A semi-colon was requested to be added between 'terrorism' and 'immigration'
- Hannes Swoboda proposed an amendment to include 'diversified gas transport routes' and 'resources'
- László Surján agreed to the amendment, but noted that it would only be valid for the amended version
- The amendment was incorporated in the first of the four votes and was rejected
- László Surján proposed replacing 'fears' in the original text with the word 'concerns' for paragraph 31
