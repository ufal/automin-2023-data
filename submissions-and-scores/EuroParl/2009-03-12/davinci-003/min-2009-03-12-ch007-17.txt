- Meeting discussed Amendment 13 regarding the informal agreement between Croatia and Slovenia
- Amendment 13 was voted on with the agreement of both Croatia and Slovenia
- Amendment 13 reads: “Recalls the informal agreement reached on 26 August 2007 by the Prime Ministers of Croatia and Slovenia on the submission of their border dispute to an international body; welcomes the readiness of Croatia and Slovenia to accept the mediation offer made by the Commission and takes the view that this mediation should be based on international law; in this context, looks forward to a rapid advancement of the accession negotiations;”
- Hannes Swoboda reported that there had been a lot of discussions between the groups over the last few days
- The amendment had the broadest agreement in the House
