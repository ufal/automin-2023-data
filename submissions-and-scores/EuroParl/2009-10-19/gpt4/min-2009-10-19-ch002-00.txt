- The European Parliament discussed the issue of airline bankruptcies and their impact on passengers.
- 77 airline bankruptcies have occurred since 2000, mainly affecting smaller companies.
- There is a need for a protective mechanism at the European level to ensure stranded passengers are not left without assistance.
- A specific legislative proposal for a kind of bankruptcy insurance is being considered.
- The European Commission is working on a solution, possibly involving a guarantee fund or insurance to protect passengers.
