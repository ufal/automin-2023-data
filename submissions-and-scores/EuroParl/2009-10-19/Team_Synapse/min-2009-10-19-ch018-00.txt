SUMMARY
- The next item is the joint debate on the proposal for a Council decision on the conclusion of the Agreement between the European Community and the Republic of Mauritius on the short-stay visa waiver - C7-0015/2009.
- The agreement covers Mauritius, Seychelles, Barbados, The Federation of St Kitts and Nevis, the Bahamas and Antigua and Barbuda.
- It applies to European Union citizens who travel to these countries and vice-versa when they travel to the European Union.
- Antigua and Barbuda, the Bahamas, Barbados, Mauritius, Saint Kitts and Nevis and the Seychelles were transferred from the negative list to the positive list.
- The agreement covers all categories of persons travelling for all purposes except for the purpose of carrying out a paid activity.
- The Confederal Group of the European United Left - Nordic Green Left is in favour of the agreement.
- PERSON114 supports Simon Busuttil's report, which endorses the European Commission's initiative.
- The vote on the issue will take place tomorrow.
- The joint debate is closed and the vote is already won't take place.
- The principle of reciprocity is very important to the European Union.
