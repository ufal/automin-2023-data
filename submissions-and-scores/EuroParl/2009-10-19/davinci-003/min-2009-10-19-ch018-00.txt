- European Commission and Parliament have worked to create a new completely private airline model
- European Commission and Parliament have proposed to introduce the Single European Sky reform ahead of schedule to provide practical support to all European airlines
- Objective is to prevent airlines from going bankrupt and protect jobs
- Objective is to safeguard citizens and ensure good airlines remain operational
- Tightening rules on setting up companies, requiring further capital and structural guarantees
- Tightening system for financial and operating accounts and spot-checks
- European surveys to analyse companies' flight policy, complaint-handling mechanism and refund procedure
- Making it easier to deal with cross-border complaints and compensation claims
