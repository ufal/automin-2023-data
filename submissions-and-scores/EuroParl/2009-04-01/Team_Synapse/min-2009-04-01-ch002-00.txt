DATE : 2023-05-02
ATTENDEES : PERSON006


SUMMARY
- More than 300 people drowned when a number of refugee boats went down in the Mediterranean off the coast of Libya last weekend.
- Some people were rescued by Egyptian and Libyan authorities, and some bodies have been recovered, but hundreds of people are still missing.
- European Parliament is deeply shocked and saddened by this event.
- The European Union has been experiencing an increase in immigration via the Mediterranean in the last two years.
