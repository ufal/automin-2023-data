- A request for the quorum to be established was made by 40 Members
- The President of the sitting asked for the opinion of at least one representative per group
- The President interpreted Rule 149(4) of the Rules of Procedure, which states that the Members who made the request for the quorum to be established shall be counted as present even if they are no longer in the Chamber
- The President checked whether the 40 signatories were present before the votes
- According to the services, 5 of them were present
- The President asked if 40 Members would stand up and make a request for the quorum to be established
- When no one stood up, the President proceeded to the vote
