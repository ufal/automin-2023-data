- The meeting discussed the tragic death of Professor Bronisław Geremek, a great Polish patriot and a true European who campaigned for democracy, liberty, human rights, and the rule of law.
- Geremek's commitment to the democratic opposition and the popular movement Solidarność was acknowledged.
- Geremek's contributions to Poland's place among free and democratic nations of Europe and its membership in the European Union were recognized.
- The meeting also mourned the death of colleague Willi Piecyk, a Member of the European Parliament since 1992 and a leading figure in the Committee on Transport and Tourism.
- The meeting observed a minute's silence in memory of those who lost their lives in tragic air disasters during the summer recess.
