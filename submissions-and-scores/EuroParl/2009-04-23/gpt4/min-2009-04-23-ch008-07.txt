- A delegation from Iraq visited the Parliament for interparliamentary meetings.
- Mr. Khalid Al Atiyah, First Vice-President of the House of Representatives of the Republic of Iraq, chaired the delegation.
- The President emphasized the encouraging progress made in Iraq on security and the rule of law.
- The European Union and Parliament will support Iraq in consolidating peace, democracy, and stability.
- The meetings aimed to strengthen the bonds between the two Parliaments.
