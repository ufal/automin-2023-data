• The Commission welcomed the adoption of the Weber report on simplified reporting and documentation requirements in the case of mergers and divisions of public limited liability companies. 
• The Commission had gone even further in its original proposal, but the adopted resolution will maintain a significant part of the savings potential. 
• The savings potential of the original Commission proposal amounts to EUR 172 million per year. 
• The adoption of the resolution will contribute to the potential for growth and help Europe on the way to recovery. 
• The agenda on administrative burden reduction is advancing.
