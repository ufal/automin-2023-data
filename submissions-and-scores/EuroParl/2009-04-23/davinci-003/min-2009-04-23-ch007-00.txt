- The meeting was to discuss the amendment of Regulation (EC) No 717/2007 (mobile telephone networks) and Directive 2002/21/EC (electronic communications). 
- The amendment was requested by the Council in order to remove an inconsistency about the exchange rates applicable for SMS caps and the cut-off limit. 
- The text to be added at the end of Article 1(4) of the original regulation was agreed with the Council. 
- The text states that the same reference exchange rates shall be applied to annually revise the value of the charges governed by Articles 4a, 4b and 6a(3) where these charges are denominated in currencies other than the euro. 
- The amendment was approved by the meeting.
