- The third meeting of the Parties to the Convention on Access to Information, Public Participation in Decision-making and Access to Justice in Environmental Matters was discussed.
- The rapporteur was not present for the debate, causing confusion and objections from some members.
- The Commission's priorities for the meeting included adopting a long-term strategic plan, stable financial arrangements, an interpretative decision on amendments, and continuation of work on public participation and access to justice.
- The Commission welcomed the participation of Members of the European Parliament at the meeting.
- The sitting was closed due to the absence of the author of the question, and a written reply was provided.
