- A delegation from the Japanese Parliament attended the 29th EP/Japan Interparliamentary Meeting in Brussels and Vienna from 2 to 6 June.
- The delegation was led by Mr. Taro Nakayama and included seven members of the House of Representatives and two members of the House of Councillors.
- The meeting focused on climate change and promoting a common approach based on the Kyoto Protocol and its successor.
- The cooperation between the European Parliament and Japan is described as fruitful, stable, and continuous.
- Both parties share values of democracy, respect for human rights, and the principle of multilateral cooperation.
