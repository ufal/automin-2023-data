- Commission triggered radioactive risk alert system following incident at Krsko in Slovenia
- Details of incident remain sketchy
- Request for transparency from European authorities
- Reactor of nuclear power plant of Krško was completely shut down on Wednesday, 4 June at 19.30
- ECURIE system requires Member States to notify Commission and other Member States of counter measures to protect population against effects of radiological or nuclear accident
- Radiation measurement data from radioactivity measuring station in Krško shows peak in radiation levels the day before alarm was sounded
