- Debate on the Commission's strategy for the third meeting of the Parties to the Convention on Access to Information, Public Participation in Decision-Making and Access to Justice in Environmental Matters (B6-0157/2008) was closed due to the absence of the rapporteur
- The Commission's priorities for the third Meeting of the Parties include:
    - Adoption of a Long-term Strategic Plan
    - Adoption of stable and predictable financial arrangements
    - Adoption of an interpretative decision by the Meeting of the Parties
    - Continuation of the work with respect to public participation and access to justice in environmental matters
    - Appointing high quality members to the Compliance Committee
- The Commission facilitates the inclusion of Members of the European Parliament as observers in Community delegations attending meetings of governing bodies of multilateral agreements
- A written reply to the question will be provided
