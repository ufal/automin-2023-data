DATE : 2023-05-02
ATTENDEES : PERSON088


SUMMARY
- The European Parliament has endorsed the establishment of a contact-point network against corruption.
- The lead committee has voted unanimously in favour of such a move.
- OLAF, Europol and Eurojust should be integral parts of this network.
- The network should submit an annual report to the European Parliament on its activities.
- The costs of all the network members should be borne from the Community budget.
