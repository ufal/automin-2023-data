- Rapporteur Alojz Peterle put seven minuses against the amendments of the Socialist Group due to credibility concerns.
- Six of the seven amendments were previously refused in the ENVI Committee.
- Peterle believes the subject of safety at work is well covered in a resolution adopted in January.
- Peterle does not see a need to repeat elements in different resolutions.
- Peterle proposes an oral amendment to reorganize the text, in agreement with the shadow rapporteurs.
