5 8.5. Aviation security measures and body scanners (vote)

Motion for a resolution B6-0562/2008

(Simple majority)

(Voting record: 'Results of votes', Item 8)

MOTION FOR A RESOLUTION

The following spoke: Manfred Weber, on behalf of the PPE-DE Group, who
requested, under Rule 170(4), that the vote be held over until the November
part-session, and Martin Schulz, on behalf of the PSE Group, who opposed
the request.

By roll-call vote (198 in favour, 337 against, and 22 abstentions),
Parliament rejected the request.

Adopted (P6_TA(2008)0521)
