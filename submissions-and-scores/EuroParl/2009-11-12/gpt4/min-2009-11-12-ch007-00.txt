- European Parliament supports the abolition of the visa regime for all Western Balkan countries.
- Citizens of the Western Balkans have been isolated by the visa regime for too long.
- Abolition of visas will contribute to regional cooperation and help build cultural, social, economic, and political bridges.
- Visa abolition for Macedonia, Serbia, and Montenegro is a positive step, but Bosnia and Herzegovina and Albania should be allowed to join as soon as they are ready.
- The European Union has a political responsibility to finalize the process of visa liberalization.
