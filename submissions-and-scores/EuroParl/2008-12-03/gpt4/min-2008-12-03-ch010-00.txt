- Meeting discussing Regulation (EC) No 1073/1999 concerning investigations conducted by the European Anti-Fraud Office (OLAF).
- Paul van Buitenen, on behalf of the Verts/ALE Group, raises concerns about recent revelations of irregularities within OLAF.
- Van Buitenen requests clarification from the Commission on whether they are considering ending the review process of the regulation.
- He asks for a statement from the Commission before the debate and vote on the Gräßle report.
- The President acknowledges the question and states that Mrs Ferrero-Waldner will have the opportunity to answer it.
