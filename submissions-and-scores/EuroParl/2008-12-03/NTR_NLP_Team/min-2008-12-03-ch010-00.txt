Meeting KeyPoints:

* The Verts/ALE group requests that the voting on the motions for a resolution regarding the participation of the state of Israel in Community programmes be postponed
* The request is based on a clear fact which was observed by the delegation from the European Parliament which travelled to Israel and visited the Palestinian territories
* No serious signs of good faith have been apparent in Israel's policy and on the ground
* I think that it would be
Following the Israeli elections in September 2019, both the Palestinians and the Israelis are more frustrated then ever before. Both sides claim that the other are failing to deliver on their obligations. Both sides believe the other side has not honoured previous agreements. The only way to create a sense of stability is to postpone a vote to review EU-Israeli relationship. The Parliament will vote in favour of a resolution that will allow negotiations between the European Union and the Israel to continue.
