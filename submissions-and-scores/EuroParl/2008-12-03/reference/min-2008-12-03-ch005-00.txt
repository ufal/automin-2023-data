5. Statements by the President

The President made the following statements:

- he condemned, on behalf of Parliament, the attacks in Mumbai on 26
November 2008, and expressed Parliament's condolences to those who had been
injured and the victims' families; he was delighted that the members of the
delegation from the INTA Committee and the European Parliament staff who
had been in Mumbai at the time of the attacks had returned unharmed;

- he condemned, on behalf of  Parliament, the assassination by ETA of the
businessman Ignacio Ura in Azpeitia (Spain), which had occurred that day.

Parliament observed a minute's silence.

The President made a statement on the occasion of the 20th anniversary of
World AIDS day on 1 December 2008.
