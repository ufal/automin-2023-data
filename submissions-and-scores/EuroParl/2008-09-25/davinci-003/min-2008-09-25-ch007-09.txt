- Meeting was about the Area of Freedom, Security and Justice (AFSJ) 2007
- Manfred Weber proposed an oral amendment for the reformulation of Amendment 1
- Amendment 1 was about voting rights for migrants in the European Union
- Weber proposed that the amendment be reworded to 'a proposal concerning the integration of long-term residents into the political life at European and local level; a step forward which could help the social, cultural and political integration of these long-term residents;'
- Objections were raised and the oral amendment was not adopted
