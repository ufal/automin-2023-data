- Meeting discussed electronic communications networks and services, protection of privacy, and consumer protection
- Commission accepted numerous amendments on recitals and articles, as well as plenary amendments
- Rapporteur Malcolm Harbour emphasized the importance of confident, well-informed consumers for electronic communications
- Viviane Reding, Member of the Commission, thanked rapporteurs and praised the European Parliament's vote for an open and competitive single market for telecom companies and consumers
- Reding highlighted the establishment of an efficient European telecom regulator and urged Parliament, Council, and Commission to work together to implement the telecoms package by 2010
