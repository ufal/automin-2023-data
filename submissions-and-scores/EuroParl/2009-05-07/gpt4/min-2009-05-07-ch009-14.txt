- The Commission supports the priority project to connect rail networks on both sides of the Alps with a high-capacity, high-speed line, mainly for goods transport.
- The project will contribute to the effective operation of the internal market, environmental targets, and economic recovery.
- EUR 786 million of funding has been authorized for priority project No 1.
- The Brenner base tunnel project has been submitted by Austrian authorities and construction permits have been issued.
- Priority project 17, the Strasbourg-Vienna high-speed link, is proceeding satisfactorily in all involved Member States, with most of the stretch between Strasbourg and Vienna to be completed by 2015.
