- The meeting discussed the need to move beyond promoting sustainability and energy efficiency and focus on creating new ways of generating energy to make cities more self-sufficient.
- Commissioner Piebalgs mentioned the development of a new energy efficiency action plan and a communication on financing low-carbon energy sources.
- The importance of a bottom-up approach and the involvement of stakeholders, such as cities, in putting forward proposals was emphasized.
- The Covenant of Mayors was highlighted as a source of intellectual input and experience for formulating policies to achieve sustainable cities.
- Topics such as smart grids, renewable energy integration, and sustainable transport were mentioned as areas of opportunity for cities to develop their own programs.
