- The Court of Justice of the European Communities ruled that airlines may not refuse to compensate passengers for flight cancellations due to technical problems.
- Member States are responsible for applying Regulation (EC) No 261/2004 on passenger rights and prosecuting non-compliant airlines.
- The Commission's role is to monitor the correct implementation of the regulation by competent national authorities.
- The Commission plans to report to Parliament and the Council on the functioning and results of Regulation (EC) No 261/2004 through a communication in the second half of the year.
- The Commission supports the privatization of airlines and the consolidation of the Community air sector, but not at the expense of competition and the effective operation of the common market.
