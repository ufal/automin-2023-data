- EU and Member States committed to pursuing gender equality and women's empowerment as a key priority in the international agenda.
- Practical implementation of gender mainstreaming in EU's external policies is weak.
- Only eight of the 27 Member States have adopted national action plans on the implementation of UN Security Council Resolution 1325.
- Women are underrepresented in high-level posts in the Commission and the Council, with no female EU special representatives currently.
- The report stresses the need for the EU to fully implement its commitments, including speeding up work on an EU action plan on gender equality.
