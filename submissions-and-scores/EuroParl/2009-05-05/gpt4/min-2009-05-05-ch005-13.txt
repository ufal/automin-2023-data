- Michael Cashman was given a mandate to negotiate with other institutions on public access to European Parliament, Council, and Commission documents.
- The negotiations were not productive, and Cashman recommends not voting and referring the decision to the next Parliament.
- The Commission will reconsider its proposal only after the two branches of legislative authority have adopted their positions.
- The Commission intends to continue a constructive dialogue with both institutions and confirms its willingness to seek a compromise with Parliament and the Council.
- Cashman emphasizes the importance of the next Parliament exercising every single prerogative it has and listening to each institution.
