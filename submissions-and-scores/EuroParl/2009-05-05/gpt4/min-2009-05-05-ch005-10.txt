- The meeting discussed petrol vapor recovery during refueling of motor vehicles.
- A good compromise was achieved at first reading after intensive negotiations with the Council and the Commission.
- The European Parliament contributed to accelerating the application of the directive by one year.
- The scope was broadened to include service stations in residential areas.
- Information and control mechanisms for service stations and citizens were strengthened.
- The application of the change will improve air quality and limit greenhouse gas emissions.
- The Commission will submit a recast proposal after a certain period for better technology and results.
