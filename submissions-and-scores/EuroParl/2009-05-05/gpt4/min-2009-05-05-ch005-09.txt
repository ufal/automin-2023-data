- Meeting discussed marketing standards for poultrymeat
- Amendments were unanimously adopted by the Committee on Agriculture and Rural Development
- Resolution rejected the Commission proposal to authorize marketing of poultrymeat for human consumption after anti-microbial treatment
- Members of the Committee on the Environment, Public Health, and Food Safety were involved in the report
- The European Commission and Council are urged to consider the report and opinion of the European Parliament for public health, food safety, and EU poultrymeat producers
