• The Commission proposed implementing measures in the current legislation to protect consumers, workers and the environment from the negative effects of nanoproducts.
• Parliament is requesting the Commission to carry out an overhaul of all relevant legislation to ensure a safe market for nanotechnology.
• The Commission has two years to comply with Parliament's request.
• Parliament almost unanimously supports the request.
• The Commission should make an immediate start on its work on an overhaul to regulate nanotechnology in a way that protects citizens.
