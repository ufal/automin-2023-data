• Nuclear energy is established in a way that each country has a nuclear safety regulator with full responsibility for implementing nuclear safety measures
• Each project submitted is analysed by the Commission and a recommendation is made on the projects to be developed
• Requirements of the environmental impact assessment must be fulfilled, as laid down in Community law
• International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEO) standards should be binding
• Commission supports cities in their efforts to promote sustainable energy through the Covenant of Mayors, Joint Research Centre, European Investment Bank, Structural Funds, and Intelligent Energy Europe programme
