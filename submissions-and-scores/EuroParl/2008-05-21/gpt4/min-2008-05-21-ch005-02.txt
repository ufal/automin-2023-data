- Parliament discusses calendar of part-sessions for 2009
- Amendment proposed regarding week 16 (13-19 April) before Orthodox Easter
- No proposal made for part-session during this week, amendment not admissible
- Mr. Daul suggests amendment to move plenary week in April 2009 to 21-24 April to respect Orthodox Easter
- Mr. Swoboda supports the oral amendment
- Mr. Nassauer raises concerns about pension rights for some Members due to the date of the constituent sitting in July 2009
- Mr. Cohn-Bendit highlights other religious and secular holidays that may be affected by the calendar
- Parliament agrees to accept the oral amendment
