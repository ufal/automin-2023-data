19. Strategy for the third meeting of the Parties to the Convention on
Access to Information, Public Participation in Decision-making and Access
to Justice in Environmental Matters (debate)

Oral question (O-0054/2008) by Miroslav Ouzk, on behalf of the ENVI
Committee, to the Commission: Commission's strategy for the third meeting
of the Parties to the Convention on Access to Information, Public
Participation in Decision-Making and Access to Justice in Environmental
Matters (B6-0157/2008)

As the author of the oral question was absent, the President decided not to
take the question.

The following spoke on that decision: Marios Matsakis, Eija-Riitta Korhola,
Ins Ayala Sender and Androula Vassiliou, Member of the Commission, who
agreed to provide a written answer to the oral question.
