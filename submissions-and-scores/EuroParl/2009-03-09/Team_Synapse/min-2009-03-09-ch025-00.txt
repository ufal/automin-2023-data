SUMMARY
- Parliament is paying closer attention to the implementation and transposal of the legislation we pass here.
- The report on the implementation of Directive 2006/43/EC on statutory audits of annual accounts and consolidated accounts is presented.
- Key definitions are framed differently in different countries and the number of Member States that have independent public oversight is a long way short of 27.
- The Commission has adopted the recommendation on quality assurance and on the limitation on the civil liability of auditors.
- The European Parliament is asking the Commission to assess the national laws transposing the directive.
