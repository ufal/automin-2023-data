- The meeting discussed an oral amendment regarding the law applicable to divorce and legal separation.
- The amendment states that spouses may agree to designate the law applicable to divorce and legal separation, provided that it is in conformity with the fundamental rights defined in the Treaties and the Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union and the principle of public policy.
- The amendment was accepted, as it limits the right of spouses to choose the law applicable to their divorce and legal separation.
- The PPE-DE Group agreed to withdraw their amendments if the oral amendment was accepted.
- The legislative resolution was discussed, with some disagreement over the acceptance of the oral amendment and the withdrawal of the PPE-DE Group's amendments.
