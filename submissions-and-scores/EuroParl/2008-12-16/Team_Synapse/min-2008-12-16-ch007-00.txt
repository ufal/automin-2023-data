SUMMARY
- The European Union and the Council are going to sign the legal acts relating to the Erasmus Mundus action programme for the enhancement of quality in higher education and the promotion of intercultural understanding through cooperation with third countries.
- The European Union is now in a position to meet the growing demand for the programme and will be able to offer European students even more financial support.
