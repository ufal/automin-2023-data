- On 26 November, a series of coordinated terrorist attacks took place in Mumbai, India, resulting in almost 190 deaths and 200 injuries.
- The European Parliament expresses outrage at the attacks and offers sympathy to the families of the victims.
- The Parliament condemns the terrorist acts and shows solidarity with India, its people, and its authorities.
- Terrorism is a direct attack on freedom, human rights, and democracy, and must be met with determined action and solidarity.
- The European Union is called upon to combat terrorism in any form together with the international community, guided by the principles of the rule of law.
