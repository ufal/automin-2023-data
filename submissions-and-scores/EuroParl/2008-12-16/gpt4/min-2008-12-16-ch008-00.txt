- Rapporteur Giuseppe Gargani proposed three oral amendments to the Conditions of Employment of Other Servants of the European Communities.
- Amendment 62: Gargani proposed deleting the parenthetic clause from 'annual amounts' up to 'annual budget' in recital 12.
- Amendment 65: Gargani proposed inserting a parenthetic clause between 'parliamentary assistance' and 'cover the total costs' in recital 12c.
- Amendment 75: Gargani proposed adding the word 'transparent' to 'reference framework' in Article 131.
- The Commission agreed on the joint text for a statute for parliamentary assistants.
- The European Parliament and the Council agreed that the transition to new conditions of employment for accredited parliamentary assistants does not require an increase in appropriations.
