- European Works Council meeting discussed an agreement reached in trialogue.
- Rapporteur Philip Bushill-Matthews raised an important point of principle regarding future dossiers.
- He emphasized that a rapporteur is appointed for Parliament as a whole, not just for the committee.
- Bushill-Matthews expressed concern about trialogues taking place without the rapporteur's support.
- He suggested that Parliament should be allowed to express its view on a report before committing to a trialogue, calling it democracy.
