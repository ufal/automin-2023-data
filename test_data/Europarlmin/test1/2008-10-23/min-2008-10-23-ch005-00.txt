5. Announcements by the President

The President informed the House that the Conference of Presidents had
decided that the 2008 Sakharov Prize be awarded to Hu Jia, defender of
human rights in China.

He also made a brief statement on the occasion of the 63rd anniversary of
the formal establishment of the United Nations on 24.10.2008. He referred
in particular to the commitment and cooperation between the European Union,
the European Parliament and the various UN programmes, and emphasised the
importance of multilateral solidarity during the current financial crisis.
