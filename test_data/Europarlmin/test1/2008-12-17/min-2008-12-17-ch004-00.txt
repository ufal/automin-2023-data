4. Fibromyalgia (written declaration)

Written declaration 0069/2008, submitted by Nicolae Vlad Popa, Jolanta
Di?kut?, Frieda Brepoels, Kathy Sinnott and Adamos Adamou, on fibromyalgia,
had been signed by the majority of Parliament's component Members.
Consequently, in accordance with Rule 116(4), it would be forwarded to its
addressees and published, together with the names of the signatories, in
the Texts Adopted of the sitting of 13.01.2009.

Abdelaziz, President of the Sahrawi Arab Democratic Republic and Secretary

on the organisation of the votes on the energy and climate change package;
