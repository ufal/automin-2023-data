9. European satellite navigation programmes (EGNOS and Galileo) ***I
(signature)

Hans-Gert Pttering (President) and Jean-Pierre Jouyet (President-in-Office
of the Council) made brief statements.

Having invited Benita Ferrero-Waldner (Member of the Commission), Angelika
Niebler (chairwoman of the ITRE Committee) and Etelka Barsi-Pataky
(rapporteur) to join him, the President, together with Jean-Pierre Jouyet,
signed the Regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council on the
further implementation of the European satellite navigation programmes
(EGNOS and Galileo), adopted on 03.07.2008 by the Council.
