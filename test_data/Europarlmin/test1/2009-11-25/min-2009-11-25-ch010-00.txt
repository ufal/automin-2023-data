10. Telecom (signature of acts)

The President and sa Torstensson (President-in-Office of the Council) made
short statements following the adoption of the 'Telecom' package.

In the presence of Carl Bildt (representative of the Council), Viviane
Reding (Member of the Commission),  Alejo Vidal-Quadras (Chair of
Parliament's delegation to the Conciliation Committee), Herbert Reul (Chair
of the ITRE Committee), Catherine Trautmann (rapporteur), Pilar del
Castillo Vera (rapporteur) and Malcolm Harbour (rapporteur), they then
signed the acts.
