2. Statement by the President

The President made a statement in which he expressed his delight at the
fact that ngrid Betancourt and 14 other hostages held by the FARC had been
liberated by the Colombian army on 2 July 2008. He had passed on
Parliament's congratulations to the people, Government and army of
Colombia, and called on all terrorist organisations, including the FARC, to
lay down their weapons and to release all hostages still captive.

                                      
                                      

The President welcomed Jean-Pierre Jouyet, the French Minister of State
responsible for European affairs, and expressed his satisfaction that the
President-in-Office of the Council was present at a Monday sitting for the
first time.
