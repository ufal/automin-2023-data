2. Statement by the President

The President made a statement in which he welcomed the signing of the act
of ratification of the Lisbon Treaty by the President of Poland, Lech
Kaczy?ski.

                                      
                                      

The President stated that a 'Question Hour' with the President of the
Commission would be held on Tuesday at 15.00.

                                      
                                      

The President made a statement on the occasion of the International Day for
the Abolition of the Death Penalty, and called on all countries applying
the death penalty to abolish it and to impose a moratorium on executions.
