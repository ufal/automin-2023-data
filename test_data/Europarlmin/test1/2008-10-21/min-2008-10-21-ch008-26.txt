26 8.26. Applicable law in matrimonial matters * (vote)

Report: on the proposal for a Council regulation amending Regulation (EC)
No 2201/2003 as regards jurisdiction and introducing rules concerning
applicable law in matrimonial matters [COM(2006)0399 - C6-0305/2006 -
2006/0135(CNS)] - Committee on Civil Liberties, Justice and Home Affairs.
Rapporteur: Evelyne Gebhardt (A6-0361/2008)

(Simple majority)

(Voting record: 'Results of votes', Item 26)

COMMISSION PROPOSAL

The following spoke: Panayiotis Demetriou, on behalf of the PPE-DE Group,
who, with the support of Evelyne Gebhardt (rapporteur), moved an oral
amendment to Amendment 38, and Carlo Casini, who stated that the oral
amendment was compatible with Amendments 32-37; the oral amendment was then
accepted.

The following spoke: Bruno Gollnisch, who requested that the text be
referred back to the committee; Parliament rejected this request.

The following spoke: Evelyne Gebhardt and Panayiotis Demetriou, on behalf
of the PPE-DE Group, who recalled that Amendments 32-37 had fallen
following approval of Amendment 38, as amended orally, and Cristiana
Muscardini, on behalf of the UEN Group, on those amendments.

Approved as amended (P6_TA(2008)0502)

DRAFT LEGISLATIVE RESOLUTION

Adopted (P6_TA(2008)0502)
